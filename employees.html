Our Team

Erick Goldberg  (Senior Vice President, Human Resources)

Erick (4).jpg Erick Goldberg Senior Vice President, Human Resources Erick Goldberg, SVP of Human Resources, first joined PetSmart in 2001 as manager of services recruiting. In 2004, he was appointed director of talent acquisition and associate relations, and in 2009, he became vice president of field Human Resources. Erick was appointed vice president of Human Resources for the store support group and distribution centers in 2012. And in February 2013, he became senior vice president of Human Resources. Prior to joining PetSmart, he was with Distribution Architects International for eight years in various leadership roles including director of Human Resources. He also previously held Human Resources leadership positions at Management Technology America and Triad Systems Corporation.

Michael Goodwin   (Senior Vice President, Chief Information Technology Officer)

mike.jpg Michael Goodwin Senior Vice President, Chief Information Technology Officer Michael Goodwin joined PetSmart in June 2014 as senior vice president and chief information officer. He has more than 20 years of information technology experience, most recently serving as senior vice president and chief information officer of Technology and Business Enablement at Hallmark. Michael began his career at Hallmark as an end-user technology analyst in 1990 and has held positions of increasing responsibility, culminating with his promotion to SVP and CIO in 2006 when he was also named a corporate officer. Prior to joining Hallmark Cards, he served as an officer in the United States Army.
