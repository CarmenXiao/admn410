About Us

Durham House of Pets

Durham House of Pets Pet Co., Ltd. is located in the beautiful Suzhou Industrial Park, next to Youjia Farm Resort. The entire site is about 40 acres, surrounded by small rivers, trees, large lawns, divided into four areas according to function: office area, kennel area, training area, leisure resort.

Durham House of Pets Pet Co., Ltd. was established in 2008. Its main business activities include: sale of dogs, pet foster care, pet training and training and rental of working dogs, and pet medical treatment. For 10 years, it has been trying its best to create a brand image, bringing high-quality products and services to customers, and as a police dog base in Suzhou City Branch to undertake security tasks. In 2009, it began serving the Inspection and Quarantine Bureau and was active at the international airport, international terminal, and international train station. It became the first dog in the national quarantine inspection and won the first place in the 2013 national quarantine dog competition. Achievements.
